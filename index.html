Plant Planet

Description:

Plant Planet is a single-player, multilevel game that progresses in difficulty over time. The player is a robot who is stranded on a strange planet where plants grow rapidly but are under constant attack by asteroids and aliens. The player’s goal is to survive by collecting plants after they grow large but before they get destroyed by enemies. Simple controls include the arrow or WASD keys to move above and around the planet’s surface, as well as the spacebar to fire ammunition to attack enemies. Enemies are spawned randomly, so the player must learn to aim quickly and accurately whenever they appear.Created with the Unity development platform, Plant Planet is available to play on PC via WebGl starting June 10, 2021. Due to the game’s easy controls and straightforward objective, Plant Planet is suitable for gamers of all ages looking for a fun challenge game!
