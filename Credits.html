Reflection

Our team did really well communicating amongst ourselves throughout the quarter. Team members made it a priority to maintain near daily correspondence informing each other of our current progress including what was successful as well as any challenges being faced. This consistent communication allowed both team members to remain motivated and exceed expectations at our individual tasks. Our collaboration can be seen in every aspect of our project, from the powerpoint presentation to the game itself. These products are the result of hours spent together in discussion dedicated to planning, revision, and execution. Being able to rely on each other for support at short notice greatly contributed to the success of our project.

If our team had an additional two-weeks to work, we would first like to expand the number of levels beyond the current three. Possibly a system where automatically generated planets for each level could be implemented and allow for infinite levels, where the game keeps going until the player loses. Ultimately, this change would make the game more interesting for skilled players who have mastered beating the first three levels. Another addition we would like to add are some player customization functionalities to further motivate the player. Implementing rewards like player outfits, new plant varieties, among others allow the player to personalize their game experience and feel more invested in the Plant Planet universe. The last item we would like to add is a high score board, so that players can keep track of their personal records and compare them with friends.
